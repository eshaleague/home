WHY BE A CAPTAIN?


  • We have already done all the work for you in the package we provide, your job is easy.
  • Low time commitment (only one hour a week).
  • Captains can participate in the league too!
  • Captains do NOT need knowledge of games to run club.
  • Captains gurantee their school's spot in the tournament.
  • Great leadership opportunity for work and university applications.
  • Our Leadership team guides you every step of the way through social media.

THE STARTUP PACKAGE


  • Shared with Captains on Google Drive.
  • All the finished files you need to run a club.
  • Includes one guide, a club proposal, posters, slideshows, and more.

CAPTAIN'S JOB


Using our startup package, captains will:

  1. Setup an esports club at their highschool
  2. Advertise to their school through posters and announcements
  3. Run the club and create teams within the club
  4. Register their teams for the season ahead

Captains will have lots of time to complete each step and will be assisted by our leadership team.

THE TOURNEY


Unlike ordinary esports leagues, schools will be represented by multiple teams and every student will have the opportunity to participate

Teams will play againt each other in an online Round Robin style tournament. The winners will be officially crowned the ESHA Champions.
