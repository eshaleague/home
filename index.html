YOUR SCHOOL NEEDS YOU


If you do not see your school above that means a member of your school has not registered as captain. High school teams are individually run by school captains. Captains have three main responsibilities: recruit team members, manage meetings, and to register their team. All schools must be represented by a team captain to participate in the league. Being captain is easy and will ensure your school's participating in the league. Please review the startup package and register as a captain below.

THE STARTUP PACKAGE


The goal of the startup package is to establish a weekly meeting location for captains and their teams. During this time crucial tasks will be completed before teams can officially register for the tournament. The package is located on Google Drive and includes forms, posters, slideshows, and an easy to follow guide. Packages will be distributed to captains when the "Teams Assemble" period begins. The Tri-City Open leadership team will do their best to make sure this process is as smooth as possible by being active on social media and checking in with captains on a weekly basis.
