WELCOME TO THE LEAGUE


The Tri-City Open is on a mission to provide a professional and competitive environment for video game enthusiasts enrolled in high school.

The millions of League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and Dota 2 players deserve an esports program dedicated to highlighting the best of individuals and team play. This tournament invites everyone to compete and represent their schools. In the end, only one school will remain victorious

YOUR JOURNEY BEGINS

HOW IT WORKS


Unlike ordinary esports leagues, the Tri City Open invites everyone to participate. Schools will be represented by multiple teams and each team will have an opportunity to earn points towards their school.

An established points system will reward points based on match results, as well as crowd pleasing moments throughout the games. The school with the most points at the end of the season will be crowned the Tri-City Open Champions.
