YOUR SCHOOL NEEDS YOU


If you do not see your school above that means your school does not have a captain yet. Captains have three main responsibilities: recruit team members, manage meetings, and to register their team. They will do this by establishing an eSports club at their school. Being captain is easy and it will ensure your school's participating in the Tri City Open. Please review the startup package below and register as a captain at the bottom of the page. The Captain's tab on our website includes everything captains will need.

THE STARTUP PACKAGE


Located on Google Drive the startup package includes forms, posters, slideshows, and a simple guide that must be followed by all captains. The goal of the startup package is to establsh a club at yours high school, recruit team members, and to complete crucial tasks before the season begins. When the "Teams Assemble" period begins packages will be distributed to Captains and further instructions will be given. The Tri-City Open leadership team will do their best to make sure this process is as smooth as possible by being active on social media and checking in with captains on a weekly basis.
