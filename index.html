YOUR SCHOOL NEEDS YOU


Welcome to the Tri-City Open: Your Premier High School eSports league! If you do not see your school above that means your school does not have a captain yet. By using the resources we provide, captains will recruit team members, manage meetings, and register their club team for the tournament. They will do this by establishing an eSports club at their school with our startup package. Being captain is easy and it will ensure your school's participating in the Tri City Open. Please review the startup package below and register as a captain at the bottom of the page. The Captain's tab on our website includes important dates and intructions for all Captains.
