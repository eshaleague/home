Season 0 Begins, Season 1 Registration now open

10/22/2017

The first ESHA season has finally started with three school clubs competing for the League of Legends. Counter-Strike, and Clash Royale championships beginning November 24. The W.Vikings and W.Crusaders have successfully created their high school clubs and their teams will be participating in season zero, with the addition of 1 team from S.Highlanders.

With the first test season in progress, signups for season 1 captains have begun. Registration will be done through an online form located on the “Captains” tab on our website.
