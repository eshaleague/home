Captain Registration Now Open

9/7/2017

School captain registration is now open on our home page! Over the next few weeks captains will setup esports clubs using the startup package we provide. The startup package (located on google drive) includes everything a high school student needs to run an esports club at their school. Our leadership team will be in constant communication with captains to make sure school clubs are set up and ready before the season begins.

The “Captains” tab on our website includes links and updated information for all captains. Captain registration closes Monday September 25 at 12am. Official Schools and captains will be announced during this time.
