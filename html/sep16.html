Captain Registration Now Closed

9/30/2017

School captain registration is now colsed. Over the next few weeks captains will be setting up esports clubs at their schools using the startup package we provide. The startup package (located on google drive) includes everything a high school student needs to run an esports club. Clubs will have multiple teams and will register for the league on November 6.

The 3 games that will be played during season 1 are League of Legends, Counter Strike: Global Offensive, and Clash Royale. Each student that participates will get their own profile page on our website which will display stats and in-game highlights during the regular season. Our website will also feature the best players from each school and students from across the province will fight for the spotlight.

The “Captains” tab on our website includes links and updated information for all captains. School clubs participating in the league will be on October 2nd. All chosen captains will also get a notification by us on social media.
