WELCOME TO THE LEAGUE


The Tri-City Open is on a mission to provide a professional and competitive environment for video game enthusiasts enrolled in high school.

The millions of League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and Clash Royale fans deserve an esports program dedicated to highlighting the best of individuals and team play. This tournament invites everyone to compete and represent their schools. In the end, only one school will remain victorious

YOUR JOURNEY BEGINS

THE TOURNEY


Unlike ordinary esports leagues, schools will be represented by multiple teams and each team will have an opportunity to earn points towards their school.

An established points system will reward points based on match results, as well as crowd pleasing moments throughout the games. The school with the most points at the end of the season will be crowned the Tri-City Open Champions. Individual awards for tournament wins will also be given.

By The Fans For The Fans

The Tri-City Open was created by a group of high school students who wanted to run their own esports tournament. They realised that competitive video games were on the rise in universities and colleges so they decided to take a different approach by targeting high school students.

The gang managed to pull it together to deliver an experience everyone can enjoy. They hope to host a professional and one of a kind tournament that will highlight the very best of students and schools across the province.
