WELCOME TO THE LEAGUE


The Electronic Sports for High School Association (ESHA) is on a mission to provide a professional and competitive environment for video game enthusiasts enrolled in high school. Our goal is to educate students on how to balance their academic and video gaming careers.

The millions of League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, and Clash Royale fans deserve an esports program dedicated to highlighting the best of individuals and team play. This tournament invites everyone to compete and represent their schools. In the end, only one school will remain victorious

By The Fans For The Fans

The ESHA was created by a group of high school students who wanted to run their own esports tournament. They realised that competitive video games were on the rise in universities and colleges so they decided to take a different approach by targeting high school students.

The gang managed to pull it together to deliver an experience everyone can enjoy. They hope to host a professional and one of a kind tournament that will highlight the very best of students and schools from across the province.
