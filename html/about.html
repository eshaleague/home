WELCOME TO THE LEAGUE


The eSports for High School Association (ESHA) is on a mission to inaugurate, establish, and unite high school clubs from across the nation under the common interest of eSports.

ESHA has created an eSports program dedicated to highlighting and celebrating the best of individuals and team play in high schools.

By The Fans For The Fans

The ESHA was created by a group of high school students who wanted to run their own esports tournament.
They hope to host a professional and one of a kind tournament that will highlight the very best of students and schools from across the nation.
