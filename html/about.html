By The Fans For The Fans

The Tri-City Open was created by a group of high school students who wanted to run their own esports tournament. Realizing that competitive video games were on the rise in universities and colleges, they decided to take a different approach by targeting high school students.

Although some were harder working than others, the gang managed to pull it together to deliver an experience everyone can enjoy. They hope to host a professional and one of a kind tournament that will highlight the very best of students and schools across the province.
