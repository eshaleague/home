THE STARTUP PACKAGE


  • Shared with Captains on Google Drive
  • All the finished files you need to run a club
  • Includes a step by step guide, a club proposal, posters, slideshows, and more

CAPTAIN'S JOB


Using our startup package Captains will:

  1. Register on our Captains page
  2. Establish a club at their highschool
  3. Advertise to their club with posters and announcements
  4. Run the club and create teams
  5. Register their teams for the season ahead

Captains will be assisted by our leadership team every step of the way.

THE TOURNEY


Unlike ordinary esports leagues, schools will be represented by multiple teams and every student will have the opportunity to participate.

Teams will play againt each other in an online style tournament. The winners will be officially crowned the ESHA Champions.
